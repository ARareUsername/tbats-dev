# Proposal: Hero Layout & Theme Overhaul

## Why

We want to elevate the visual aesthetics and brand positioning of tbats.dev to make the site look and feel even more premium, professional, and visually engaging. By shifting the hero section to a left-aligned, full-screen layout with serif typography and floating metrics cards, we command visual authority and highlight our Pampanga identity. Adding consistent theme components and visual drop-down icons reinforces a polished, premium experience across the entire landing page.

## What Changes

- **100vh Full Screen Hero**: Restructure the hero layout to cover the entire initial viewport, left-aligning the typography and introducing a floating graphics layout on the right.
- **Serif Typography**: Transition the hero title to the premium `Playfair Display` serif font.
- **Local Identity & Copy**: Revise the hero text to read `"Pampanga Web Design Company. Crafting Digital Experiences That Convert."`
- **Floating Overlapping Visual Elements**: Render three floating card overlays (Google rank, traffic graph, and +12 Sales count) with smooth hover and float animations on the right side of the hero.
- **Mount Arayat Backdrop**: Introduce a custom dark vector image of Mount Arayat as the background of the Bento Box Services section to emphasize local expertise.
- **Bento Card Text & Graphic Update**: Expand the 1-on-1 support description text and enlarge the monospace code graphic block.
- **Dropdown Menu Icons**: Enhance the dropdown menus under Services and Our Work with colorful, consistent inline icons representing each destination.
- **Theme Consistency**: Perform a global audit and adjustments on margins, cards, borders, and button elements for ultimate light/dark consistency.

## Capabilities

- **Hero Section Overhaul**: Full viewport hero grid layout, serif titles, and floating cards.
- **Bento Services Enhancements**: Pampanga photo/vector backdrop, expanded graphics, and updated text.
- **Dropdown Menu Icons**: Colored icons for navbar dropdown links.
- **Theme Consistency**: Harmonious cards, borders, and buttons across all pages.
